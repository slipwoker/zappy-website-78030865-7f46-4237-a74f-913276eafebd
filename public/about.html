    The 1iT Story: Building IT Infrastructure Solutions for Over 30 Years

    Founded in the early 1990s, 1iT has been at the forefront of Israel's technology revolution. We began our journey as a small IT support company, helping businesses navigate the complexities of emerging computer networks and enterprise software. Over three decades, we've evolved into a comprehensive managed service provider (MSP), delivering everything from Google Cloud services to advanced cybersecurity solutions.

    Our growth mirrors the transformation of business technology itself. We've witnessed and participated in every major shift—from client-server architectures to cloud computing, from basic email systems to sophisticated collaboration platforms like OneDrive and Google Workspace, from simple firewalls to AI-powered threat detection. This experience gives us unique insights into what works, what doesn't, and how to build IT infrastructure that truly serves business needs.

    Today, 1iT serves hundreds of organizations across Israel, from innovative startups to established enterprises, law firms to high-tech companies. What hasn't changed in 30 years is our commitment to proactive service, technical excellence, and building long-term partnerships with our clients. We don't just fix problems—we prevent them. We don't just deploy technology—we ensure it drives real business results.

    As a leading managed service provider, we've built strategic partnerships with the world's top technology companies: Microsoft, Google, Acronis, SentinelOne, and Fortinet. These relationships enable us to deliver enterprise-grade solutions with the support and resources of global leaders, while maintaining the personal service and responsiveness of a local partner.
